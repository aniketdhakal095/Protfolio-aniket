Flowers in my Garden

Flower Brifing

Flowers are beautiful and fragrant plants that belong to the angiosperm division of the plant kingdom. They have bright and colorful petals that attract pollinators like bees, butterflies, and hummingbirds. Flowers play an important role in the reproduction of plants and come in a variety of shapes and sizes. They are also used for ornamental purposes, for perfumes, and for religious and cultural ceremonies.

There are more than 80+ plants and 25+ trees in my garden, some of them are fruit some are normal trees. I got this hobby from my grandpa who worked in agricultural sector for years before I was born. I just want to work in our field with him to make it a little bit easier for him.
