What are Certificates?

Certificates are official documents that recognize an individual's achievements, skills, or education. They provide proof of completion and demonstrate a level of competence in a specific field. Certificates can be earned through educational institutions, online courses, or through professional organizations. They can serve as valuable credentials for job seekers and help individuals stand out in the competitive job market.
